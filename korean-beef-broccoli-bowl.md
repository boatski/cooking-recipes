# Korean Beef & Broccoli Bowl

## Information
Servings: 6
Calories: 470
Macros:
* Carbs: 32g
* Fat: 22g
* Protein: 35g

## Ingredients
* 2lb 90% ground beef
* 1 cup brown rice, uncooked
* 10oz broccoli florets
* 4 garlic cloves, minced
* 3 green onions, thinly sliced
* 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
* 1/2 cup low sodium soy sauce
* 2 tbsp ground ginger
* 4 tsp sesame oil
* 2 tsp sriracha
* 4 tbsp olive oil
* 1/2 tsp sesame seeds


## Directions
### Preparation
Rinse the rice thoroughly. Mince the garlic. Chop the green onions. Whisk the brown sugar, soy sauce, ginger, sesame oil and Sriracha in a small bowl.

### Cooking
Add olive oil to a large skillet on medium heat. Add garlic and cook for one minute. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in soy sauce mixture and green onions. Simmer for two minutes.

Split everything into six containers.
